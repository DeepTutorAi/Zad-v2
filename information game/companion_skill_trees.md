# Companion Skill Trees (The Bond of Allies)

## Core Concept
- **Simplified System:** Companions have a more focused skill tree compared to the player. Each companion has **2 distinct paths** to choose from.
- **Structure:** Each companion has **24 skills** in total, presented in **12 Tiers**.
- **The Choice:** At each Tier, the player can choose **1 of 2 available skills** for the companion.
- **Max Skills:** A companion can learn a maximum of **12 skills**.
- **Requirements:** Skills require the companion to reach a certain **Level**. Stat requirements are determined by their class and are automatically met as they level up.
- **Auto-Leveling:** When a companion levels up, the player will be prompted to choose a new skill if a new Tier is unlocked.
