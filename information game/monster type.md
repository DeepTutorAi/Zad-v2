# Monster Type System - Dark Fantasy RPG

## Monster Type Categories (8 Types)

### 1. **HUMANOID** (มนุษย์/สัญชาติมนุษย์ไว้)
**Characteristics:** Bipedal, use weapons/tools, can be reasoned with

**Examples:**
- Bandit / Thug (Low-tier)
- Corrupted Knight (Mid-tier)
- Dark Sorcerer (Mid-tier)
- Cult Fanatic (High-tier)

**Strengths Against:**
- Mage (Simple AI, predictable patterns)
- Shaman (No magic immunity)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Knight (Parry/Counter mechanics designed for humanoid combat)
- Rogue (Exploit weak points, backstab)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Can surrender/flee if health < 20%
- Some have Treasure Chests
- Dialogue options available (Intimidate/Persuade)

---

### 2. **BEAST** (สัตว์ร้าย)
**Characteristics:** Quadrupedal or animalistic, instinct-driven, territorial

**Examples:**
- Dire Wolf Pack (Low-tier)
- Armored Boar (Mid-tier)
- Corrupted Bear (Mid-tier)
- Ancient Basilisk (High-tier)

**Strengths Against:**
- Ranger (Can rush through arrows if close enough)
- Mage (Low INT, no spell vulnerability)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Ranger (Ranged engagement keeps distance safe)
- Shaman (Nature control, can calm/debuff)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Pack behavior: Weaker when alone, stronger in groups
- Bleed status: Physical weapons cause extra damage
- Some have Hide/Fur (Armor)

---

### 3. **AERIAL** (ปักษ์/บินได้)
**Characteristics:** Flying, mobile, hard to melee, ranged attacks from sky

**Examples:**
- Wyvern (Mid-tier) - Dive attacks
- Corrupted Griffin (Mid-tier) - Dual damage (talons + magic)
- Storm Raven (Low-tier) - Lightning projectiles
- Sky Leviathan (High-tier) - AOE breath attacks

**Strengths Against:**
- Shaman (Limited movement options)
- Paladin (Slower combat style)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Knight (Shield Bash interrupts takeoff, grounds them)
- Ranger (Arrows don't miss flying targets)

**Special Mechanics:**
- First turn: Often takes to air (can't melee hit)
- Dice roll to force landing (Dex check or special ability)
- Takes reduced damage from Magic (flies too high)

---

### 4. **UNDEAD** (ผีดิบ/กำพร้า)
**Characteristics:** No vital organs, immune to poison/bleed, weak to holy

**Examples:**
- Zombie (Low-tier) - Slow, tanky
- Skeletal Knight (Mid-tier) - Armed with weapons
- Wraith (Mid-tier) - Incorporeal, hard to hit
- Lich King (High-tier) - Magic caster + undead durability
- **Patient Zero** (High-tier) - Plague spreader (Unique Boss)
- **Bloodbound Gatekeeper** (High-tier) - Shadow guardian (Unique Boss)

**Strengths Against:**
- Rogue (Poison doesn't work, bleed immunity)
- Beast-focused classes

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Paladin (Holy magic does bonus damage)
- Warlock (Dark doesn't help vs undead, but Curse still works)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Poison/Bleed damage: REDUCED or IMMUNE
- Can regenerate if Corpse nearby
- Holy light: Stun effect (FAI scaling)

---

### 5. **FORMLESS/ETHEREAL** (ปีศาจ/วิญญาณ)
**Characteristics:** No physical body, incorporeal, immune to physical damage mostly

**Examples:**
- Ghost / Spirit (Low-tier) - Haunting phantoms
- Shadow Entity (Mid-tier) - Dark incorporeal mass
- Ethereal Shade (Mid-tier) - Phasing attacks
- Void Wraith (High-tier) - Existential threat

**Strengths Against:**
- Knight (Armor useless, physical immunity)
- Ranger (Arrows pass through)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Mage (Magic pierces ethereal form)
- Warlock (Dark energy solidifies them temporarily)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Physical damage: 0% or 10% reduction only
- Magic damage: Normal/full
- Can pass through walls (escape routes)
- Intangibility: Dodge melee attacks automatically (need Magic/Special skill)

---

### 6. **ELEMENTAL** (ธาตุ/ปีศาจธาตุ)
**Characteristics:** Made of pure element (Fire/Ice/Lightning), weak to opposite element, immune to own element

**Examples:**
- Fire Golem (Low-tier) - Imbues fire on attacks
- Ice Elemental (Mid-tier) - Freeze/slow aura
- Lightning Sprite (Mid-tier) - Chain lightning
- Lava Drake (High-tier) - Fire immunity + AOE burn
- **Magma Mother** (High-tier) - Dragon/Elemental hybrid (Unique Boss)

**Strengths Against:**
- Anyone without elemental damage
- Can be healed by own-element damage (risk/reward)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Mage (Elemental magic counter)
- Shaman (Elemental switching exploits weakness)

**Special Mechanics:**
- **Fire Golem:** Weak to Water/Ice spells, immune to Fire
- **Ice Elemental:** Weak to Fire/Lightning, immune to Ice
- **Lightning Sprite:** Weak to Ground/Dark, immune to Lightning
- Summon reinforcement if not defeated quickly

---

### 7. **PLANT/NATURE** (พืช/ธรรมชาติ)
**Characteristics:** Rooted or slow-moving, regenerates, multi-part body

**Examples:**
- Overgrown Vine Creeper (Low-tier) - Grapple attacks
- Thorned Treant (Mid-tier) - Tanky, multiple limbs
- Carnivorous Bloom (Mid-tier) - Poison pollen
- Ancient Root Horror (High-tier) - Summon adds, regen

**Strengths Against:**
- Flying enemies (Constrained by vines)
- Mage (Magic not super effective)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Ranger (Fire arrows/Physical pierce)
- Warlock (Curse/Dark withers plant life)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Rooted: Can't move first 1-2 turns (must act from place)
- Regenerate: 10% HP per turn (need burst damage or fire)
- Multi-part: Can attack limbs separately or root

---

### 8. **CONSTRUCT/MECHANICAL** (เครื่องจักร/สิ่งก่อสร้าง)
**Characteristics:** No vital organs, strategic weak points (core/joints), no status effects

**Examples:**
- Golem Sentinel (Low-tier) - Slow, heavy armor
- Clockwork Knight (Mid-tier) - Fast, complicated
- Runed Cannon (Mid-tier) - Stationary, high damage projectiles
- **Cursed Scarecrow** (Mid-tier) - Possessed construct (General Boss)
- Void Machine (High-tier) - Sentient, adapts

**Strengths Against:**
- Poison/Status effect users (Immune)
- Unsure strategy (Rigid patterns)

**Weaknesses Against:**
- Rogue (Find weak points, high Crit damage)
- Mage (Disruption magic destroys circuitry)

**Special Mechanics:**
- Weak points: Head/Core/Joints (extra damage)
- Status immunity: Poison/Bleed/Curse don't work
- Overload: Take damage when casting too many spells nearby
- Malfunction: High-Crit can jam parts (reduce action)
